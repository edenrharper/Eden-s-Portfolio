The Best Movies According to Eden

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Inside Out 2

This is my favorite Pixar movie at the moment. I really loved how it showed us how all emotions are good for you, even the ones that we don't think are super great.

High School Musical

I know this movie might be considered a bit old, but it is just a classic.

Descendants Rise of the Red

I am a fan of Descendants and I was really excited when they released a fourth one. Despite them introducing all new characters, I thought it was really good overall.
